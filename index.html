Wha'gwan Film Festival

Spring 2021

The WHA’GWAN FILM FESTIVAL is happening in Spring 2021

And we’re looking for YOUR films to feature.

Are you aged 14-21, and live in London?

Can you show us WHA’GWAN in your life on film?

Then we want YOU and YOUR FILMS!

Films can be anything from 30 seconds up to 30 minutes long, and the subject is YOU and YOUR LIFE.

Literally, TELL US WHA’GWAN!

The films should tell a story or give a glimpse into your life.

You Could:

  • -Create a piece to show how BLM affected you
  • -Write a poem about your hopes and dreams
  • -Showcase what race means in your family
  • - Film a scene based on your favourite music track
  • -Record a dance piece, performed on your local streets
  • -Use comedy to express your opinion
  • -Explore your identity and what it means to be you
  • -Or something else….

You can film on your phone, so there’s no need for fancy equipment.

And if you want to make more than one film…. feel free. It’s YOUR STORY, however you want to tell it!
